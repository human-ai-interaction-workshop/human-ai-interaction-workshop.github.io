ICML 2026 Workshop Proposal — Submitted

Foundations of Human–AI Interactions:
Theory, Algorithms, Practice

AI is increasingly experienced as an interactive system—not a static predictor. This workshop proposal focuses on the science of interaction: human factors (reliance, trust calibration, cognitive effort, decision quality) and the algorithmic foundations needed to design interactive AI responsibly.
